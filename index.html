OUR STORY

Founded in the heart of Las Cruces, Wildwest Smokeshop has been serving the Mesilla Valley community with premium products and expert knowledge since our opening. We're more than just a dispensary - we're a destination for cannabis enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

Our team at the Mesilla Valley Mall location is dedicated to providing exceptional service, quality products, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or exploring cannabis for the first time, we're here to guide you.
